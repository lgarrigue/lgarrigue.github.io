Louis Garrigue

Postdoctoral researcher in mathematical quantum mechanics


    I am a postdoctoral researcher in mathematical quantum physics and numerical analysis at Stuttgart's university, in Benjamin Stamm's team. You can find my CV here, and email me on louis.garrigue@ians.uni-stuttgart.de

    My research interest is mathematical quantum mechanics, and I work with functional and numerical analysis.
